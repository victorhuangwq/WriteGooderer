How to test

  1. Build the extension with npm run build, then load dist/ at chrome://extensions.
  2. Open this page in Chrome and click into any field below.
  3. The "W" button should appear on the four cards below, and stay hidden on the negative control.
  4. Click "W" to open the popup, then try Proofread or Change Tone.
